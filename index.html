Tribute to Robin Williams

Stand-up Comedian and Actor

The life and Death of Robin Williams

A kind, loving soul, who was loved loved by many! The report of Robin Williams' suicide was a shock to all. Nobody ever guessed that this person who was always cracking a joke and smiling could fall victim to suicide. His death has taught so many that although someone may smile and laugh, doesn't mean that they aren't concealing something darker that maybe hurting them on the inside.

TimeLine

- 1951

Robin Williams was born on July, 21, 1951 in Chicago, Illinois.

- 1973

Williams was accepted into Julliard.

- 1975

Robin Williams begins his stand-up comedy career.

- 1978

Williams landed his first major TV role (Mork ;& Mindy), in the same year he also marries Valerie Velardi.

- 1980

Williams landed his first movie role in the film Popeye.

- 1986

Williams founded Comic Relief USA.

- 1987

Williams stars in Good Morning, Vietnam.

- 1989

Williams stars in Dead Poets Society and in the same year marries Marsha Garces.

- 1991 - 2002

Williams stars in various films, including Hook, Aladdin, Jumanji, Patch Adams, Good Will Hunting, What Dreams May Come, Bicentennial Man and Insomnia.

- 2006

Williams stars in Night at the Museum.

- 2007

Williams' brother passes away.

- 2009

Williams is hospitalized for Heart Surgery, he also starred in World's Greatest Dad.

- 2011

Broadway acting debut - Marries Susan Schneider.

- 2014

Williams dies by suicide on Aug 11, 2014.
